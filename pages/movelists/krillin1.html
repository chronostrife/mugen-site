Character Information:

Krillin(Kuririn) is Goku's childhood friend and one of the most loyal and dependable fighters for Earth. Despite having nearly every bone in his body broken, being killed by demons, Freeza, and Buu and being outclassed in battle power throughout all of DBZ, he still lends his efforts willingly to protect the peace and proves himself as one of the strongest humans on Earth.

During the Android and Cell sagas Krillin lends aids the rest of the team in hunting and challenging the androids, though he is the only Z-Fighter who questions their motives. He learns that aside from their hard coded desire to fight Goku, they lack the evil the prophesied androids were described as having. As a result, he works to help both the androids and Z-Fighters in their battles against Cell. He would eventually marry and start a family with Android 18.

Krillin's specialty is his ability to manage and control his Ki for unique and effective attacks. Despite always trailing behind the saiyans and Piccolo in terms of raw power, he continues to surprise both his allies and enemies with unique skills. Using controllable Ki Attacks, Ki Discs capable of cutting through anything(besides Perfect Cell), and concetrating multiple Ki sources into one and splitting them apart again. All of his attacks are very powerful but they suffer from massive weaknesses as well, such as being slow or easily dodged.


Krillin by Chrono_Strife is designed for MUGEN 1.1. Due to the nature of the code in the AIR and CNS as well as some of the contents of the SFF, I cannot guarantee this character will work in older versions of MUGEN.

Voice Options: This character include both English(default) voice acting by Sonny Strait and Japanese voice acting by Mayumi Tanaka.
Enable Japanese VA by pointing to "Krillin_CStrife/Krillin_Jp_CStrife.def" instead of the "Krillin_CStrife" in your MUGENs select.def. Or you can simply comment out the "sound" reference to the "*Eng*.SND" and removing the comment on the *Jpn*.SND in this characters default .DEF.

This character was mostly completed in 2010, I failed to release him publicly simply due to lack of unique intro/win poses for each of his costumes. I revisited him on occasion to test out some FX and programming ideas as I worked on 16 and Garlic Jr, and finally decided to finish him in mid-2015. The original SB style Cell Saga Sheet was converted from BuYuu Retsudan Krillin by Neocide in the early 2000's, then further updated and converted to both Buu Saga(2003) and Saiyan Saga(2011) by Chrono_Strife. This character will likely have the SSF split into 3 in a later update and the user can pick which version they'd want in their Mugen.

Krillin is intended to be a balanced fighter uses a mix of melee and ki attacks to defeat his enemies. His unique trait is Ki Control, many of his ki attacks are either aimed/aimable, homing, or piercing attacks. Since he is also a martial artist he needed an emphasis in melee style attacks as well. Krillins reach is poor due to his size, however he has a variety of basics and specials that chain into one another which deal decent damage, generate some power, and create distance between him and his opponent so he can follow-up with one of his speciality ki based supers.

Additional Basics:

Dash/Air Dash
-or- -or-

Holding down the direction button will force the dash to last longer. Consecutive Air Dashes will consume power over time.

Super Jump
-or- -or-

Jump from a Crouched state. Leap greater distances and perform longer aerial combos. Can be chain into from Launcher.

Power Up

Generates power over time.

Zanzoken

Can only be performed while Dashing and Air Dashing.
Used to create or close gaps as well as dodge powerful attacks.

Advance Guard

Can only be used while block an attack.
Knocks the opponent far away.

Recovery Flip
-or-

Use to land from falls that are recoverable.

Scatter Shot Retreat

Leap backwards from lying on the ground and release a volley of weak energy blasts.

Launcher

Launches the opponent airborne. Can be follow-ed up with a super jump, air-combos, or Intantaneous Chains.

Instantaneous Chain Combo

Chains up to 4 attacks.
Can be used as a chain after Throw, and Launcher.

Throws and Grabs:

Throw

Can be performed in close proximity to the opponent. Chains into Destructo Disc or Instantaneous Chain Combo.

Specials:

Head Butt Upwards

A mid-strength attack that launches the player forward and into the air.

Head Butt Downwards

A mid-strength attack that sends the player smashing into the ground. Can be chained from Head Butt Upwards

Flip Kick

A lunge forward into a versatile flip kick for a quick burst of damage. Can be chained from Head Butt Upwards and Orin Kick Combo (after 3rd kick)

Orin Kick Combo

Slowly rise upwards while delivering a repeated weak kick, followed by a final strong rising kick. Chains into Overhead Smash -or- Flip Kick.

Overhead Smash

A standard follow-up to Orin Kick Combo

Ki Repeater

A long-ranged but weak attack.
Tapping punch will launch up to 3 blasts in succession then ends with a powerful Destructo Disc.

Destructo Disc
-or-

A powerful ranged chain attack. Can be chained from Throw and Ki Repeater. Each chain command is listed respectively.

KamehameHa Up

A strong but expensive anti-air attack. Tapping kick will launch up to 3 KamehameHas in succession.

KamehameHa Down

A strong but expensive ranged aerial attack. Tapping kick will launch up to 3 KamehameHas in succession.

Solar Flare

A flash of blinding light stuns the enemy briefly. Uses alot of power.

Orin Repeater

An extended basic combo attack that ends with a powerful kick. The first button signifies the start of what is required to finish the combo, this combo can be preceded by other basic attacks to build an even longer combo!

Supers:

Double Tsuihikidan

Krillin creates 2 energy balls that chase his opponent. They can be bounced, deflected by projectiles, and can hit the ground due to their slow turning.

Kamehameha

Fire a power energy wave. Hold Up or Down before firing to project the wave up or down.

Chain Destructo Disc

Krillin throws 3 consecutive energy discs. If 1 is easy to dodge, then surely 3 will be tougher!

Kakusan Kikou Ha

Krillin launches a slow moving energy ball in the sky, splits it, and rains it on to his opponent. This attack is devastating but easily avoidable.


Fighting Tips:

1. When using the Ki Repeater special attack, continue tapping any 'P' button to chain up to 3 energy blasts and once more for Destructo Disc!
2. Instantaneous Chain Combo (Teleport Attacks) can be used:
--after a successful Throw.
--after a successful Launcher
3. Avoid using power/energy consuming attacks when your power is low to avoid tiring out.
4. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
5. Attacks do less damage after each successful hit in a combo. After 10 hits you can only do 20% damage per attack. Supers are not affected by this.
6. Use air dash wisely, excessive use causes energy loss. When out of power you may only use a single air dash before landing.
7. Use Advance Guard if you are caught blocking in a corner with little chance of escape.
8. Recover from certain falling states by pressing PP or KK.
9. Press PP to Scatter Shot Retreat while lying down.
10. Destructo Disc can be chained after Throw for extra damage at the cost of power.
