Character Information:

Android 18 is the hot blonde of DBZ, together with her twin brother 17 they wreak havoc on the world in Trunks' time. With Goku already dead, their primary purpose was gone, and so Goku's friends and the whole planet would face their destructive play, as they satisfied their boredom killing entire cities of people.
Their strength only matched by Gohan who would eventually be killed by the twins, and Trunks who would finally end their reign of terror.

In our DBZ's time, 18 ends up being consumed by Cell. Eventually 18 is spat up by Cell thanks to Gohan, and rescued by Krillin, who's compassion eventually wins 18 over. Krillin and 18 have a child together named Marron, and they live happily ever after... until the Buu Saga and GT ruin everything! >=(

This character is meant to follow Android Saga/Alternate Future Style 18. With some obvious additions by me. Her play style follows my old SB/Budokai/MVC hybrid style, but overhauled with more of a Melty Blood feel now. Her physical and ki attacks are sort of weak, but she makes up for it with speed, and the ability to spam attacks.
18's AI is currently a bit overpowered from where I'd like it to be. I want her AI to be better than my Insect Cell, but worse than Garlic Jrs.

Chains and Fighting Tips:

1. When using the "Fireball Chain" special attack, continue tapping any 'P' button to chain up to 9 fireballs and finish with a big energy blast.
2. After throwing the opponent using the "Grab and Toss" throw, perform the "Fireball Chain" attack to juggle your opponent with energy blasts!
3. Try using the "Fireball Chain" special after a successful "Headbutt" (during the flip).
4. The "Flame Kick" special bounces the opponent off a nearby wall, you can use this advantage to attack. (Hint: Firey Angel Spear)
5. The "Flip and Slam" throw will bounce the opponent off the ground when this happens, try throwing a destructo disc.
6. Repeat the "Fire Ball Split" command shortly after the first time and you will launch an energy blast.
7. Hold down any 'K' after using the "Sliding Fire Sweep" command to power it up for a greater slide distance.
8. After almost any attack that launches your opponent(except for throws, and the uppercut launcher) you can perform teleport attacks!
8a. Teleport attack commands:
--Hold Up+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Back+ Tap 2 Kick buttons simultaneously.
-Note: Teleport attacks will change and be added to in a future update.
