Character Information:

Trunks is the half-human, half-saiyan son of Vegeta and Bulma. Trunks is a time traveler who comes from a timeline where Goku died from illness and all other Z Warrior have been killed by menacing Androids. His mission was to go to the past to save Goku and warn him of Earth's impending doom and assist them on the day of the Androids awakening.

Upon Trunks' return to the past to assist the battle against the Androids, he informed everyone that the future remained unchanged and that the timeline has already been distorted. Now there are more Androids hunting Goku and an even more malevolent being known as Cell who also came from a future that Trunks once fought to preserve.




TrunksMv9 2.0 by JediStardust, Benhazard, and Chrono_Strife is designed for MUGEN 1.1 running in OpenGL RenderMode (set in the mugen.cfg). Due to the nature of the code in the AIR and CNS as well as some of the contents of the SFF, I cannot guarantee this character will work in older versions of MUGEN.

Voice Options: This character includes both Japanese(default) voice acting and English.
Enable English VA by putting "TrunksMv9_CStrife/TrunksMv9_En_CStrife.def" instead of "TrunksMv9_CStrife" in your select.def. Or you can simply comment out(';') the "sound" reference to the "*Jp*.SND" and removing the comment on the *En*.SND in this characters default .DEF.

This is the 3rd version of the Trunks (Movie 9) character also called Trunkz. This one is completely redone as far as sprite go. I also wrote ton of new code intende to be used as a template for all future character to speed up development in the future for a potential full game. The final planned version of Trunks is the same sprite style and includes his sword, but the transformation will be removed.

Chains and Fighting Tips:

1. When using the Ki Repeater, continue tapping any P button to chain more energy blasts. Each repeat will consume even more energy.
2. Instantaneous Chain Combo (Teleport Attacks) can be used:
--after a successful Throw.
--after a successful Launcher
3. Avoid using energy consuming attacks when your power is low to avoid tiring out.
4. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
5. Attacks do less damage after each successful hit in a combo. After 10 hits you can only do 20% damage per attack. Supers are not affected by this.
6. Use air dash wisely, excessive use causes energy loss. When out of power you can only use a single air dash before landing.
7. Use Advance Guard if you are caught in a corner and only able to block.
8. Recover from certain falling states by pressing PP or KK.
