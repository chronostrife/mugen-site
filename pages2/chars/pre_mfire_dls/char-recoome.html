Recoome



Name: Recoome
Base: BuYuu Retsudan, UB22
Sprites: Neocide, Chrono_strife
Programming: Chrono_Strife
Completion: 2.1
Mugen Version: WinMUGEN
Comments: Recoome is the first grappler type I've made. He features a variety of throws and slams as well as his signature goofiness. His Melee hurts, and his ki is powerful too, and he can takes punche like a brick wall. His real draw back is his speed. Many of his attacks have a charge up time, as he strikes poses for his adoring fan.

Known Issues: His fireball chain might be removed in a future version.
