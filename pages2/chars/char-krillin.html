Comments: Krillin is balanced fighter with emphasis on Ki Control and fighting technique. His unique Ki abilities allow him to create multiple homing or aimable projectiles and use the KameHameHa with skill and style. While Krillin is a highly skilled fighter, moments of desperation may cause him to use attacks that open him up to vulnerabilities. He is short, but has limited range on his melee attacks.
