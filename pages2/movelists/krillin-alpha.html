Character Information:

Krillin(Kurrin, Kuririn) is Goku's best friend and one of the most loyal and dependable fighters for Earth. Krillin is known as being one of the strongest humans on Earth along with Tien and Yamcha. Despite having nearly every bone in his body broken, being killed by King Piccolo's demons, Freeza, and Buu and being outclassed in battle power throughout all of DBZ. He still lends his efforts willingly to protect the peace.
During the Android and Cell saga's Krillin lends his help in defending the seemingly evil androids. He would eventually marry Android 18, and have a child with her.
Krillin's specialty is his ability to manage and control his Ki. He achieve's a level of this skill even beyond what the Saiyans can do. Using controllable Ki Attacks, Ki Discs capable of cutting through anything(besides Perfect Cell), and concetrating multiple Ki sources into one and splitting them apart again. All of his attacks are very powerful if used correctly, but they suffer from massive weaknesses as well, such as being destroyed or dodged very easily.

Chains and Fighting Tips:
1. When using the "Fireball Chain" special attack, continue tapping any 'P' button to chain up to 3 fireballs and a Destructo Disc.
2. Teleport attack commands:
--Hold Forward+ Tap a+x buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Back+ Tap a+x buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Up+ Tap a+x buttons simultaneously. May not be chained immediately after launcher.
--Hold Down+ Tap a+x buttons simultaneously, This cannot be chain with by other Teleport Attacks.
3. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
4. Krillin's attacks do less damage after each successful hit in a combo. After 10 hits he will only do 20% damage per attack. Supers are not affected by this.
6. Use air dash wisely, excessive use causes energy loss. When out of power you may only use a single air dash before landing.
