Character Information:

Garlic Jr, a Makyan demon lord, seeks to rule the world by taking Kami's throne as god of Earth he would reshape the world to serve him, ruling with an army of demons. Aside from being pure evil, ruthless, and totally insane; Garlic Jr has been granted immortality by the Eternal Dragon. After a failed attempt by Kami himself, Goku, Piccolo, Krillin, and Gohan defeated Garlic Jr by sealing him away in the Dead Zone, one of Garlic Jr's own attacks.

Years have past since Garlic Jr's defeat, and his power has grown due to the Makyo Star passing close to Earth. With his increased power he breaks free of the Dead Zone and immediately attempts to dethrone Kami again, he is interrupted by Piccolo, Gohan, and Krillin. Out of frustration Garlic Jr reopens the Dead Zone. When Gohan destroyed the Makyo Star, Garlic Jr fell into the Dead Zone once more, where he would be locked away for eternity.

He returns once again to attack your Mugen characters!



Garlic Jr by Benhazard and Chrono_Strife is designed for MUGEN 1.1. Due to the nature of the code in the AIR and CNS as well as some of the contents of the SFF, I cannot guarantee this character will work in older versions of MUGEN.

Voice Options: This character include both English(default) voice acting by Chuck Huber and Japanese voice acting by Shigeru Chiba.
Enable Japanese VA by pointing to "GarlicJr2_CStrife/GarlicJr2_Jp_CStrife.def" instead of the "GarlicJr2_CStrife" in your MUGENs select.def. Or you can simply comment out the "sound" reference to the "*En*.SND" and removing the comment on the *Jp*.SND in this characters default .DEF.

This is the 3rd version of the Garlic Jr character, including updated sprites by Benhazard which are a drastic improvement over the previous versions used in Garlic Jr 1.5, which was already a huge improvement over version 1.0. The head/face of Garlic was created by Roken. Garlic Jr 2.0 should have all the gameplay changes I've made up to 16 as well as some new standards I've made for myself in terms of both graphics, code, and gameplay. Although viewing the code might be jarring and inconsistent as he IS a direct update from the 2008 and 2010 iterations.

Additional Basics:

Dash/Air Dash
-or- -or-

Holding down the direction button will force the dash to last longer. Consecutive Air Dashes will consume power over time.

Super Jump
-or- -or-

Jump from a Crouched state. Leap greater distances and perform longer aerial combos. Can be chain into from Launcher.

Power Up

Generates power over time.

Zanzoken

Can only be performed while Dashing and Air Dashing.
Used to create or close gaps as well as dodge powerful attacks.

Advance Guard

Can only be used while block an attack.
Knocks the opponent far away.

Recovery Flip
-or-

Use to land from falls that are recoverable.

Launcher

Launches the opponent airborne. Can be follow-ed up with a super jump, air-combos, or Intantaneous Chains.

Instantaneous Chain Combo

Chains up to 3 times.
Can be used as a chain after Throw, Launcher, and fully-charged Power Kick.

Throws and Grabs:

Face Breaker

Can be performed in close proximity to the opponent.

Throw

Can be performed in close proximity to the opponent. Chains into Crushing Tackle or Instantaneous Chain Combo.

Crushing Tackle

Chains from Throw.

Specials:

Shoulder Bash

A mid-strength attack the can quickly close and create gaps.

Garlic Dash

Chains from Shoulder Bash. Dash forward and Knee the opponent into the air.

Garlic Smash

Chains from Garlic Dash. Smash the opponent with your fist!

Hellfire Kick

A slow but very strong attack. Grants 1 stack of super armor.
Successful hits can be chained into Makyo Meteor.

Makyo Meteor

A powerful aerial attack. Can be chained from Hellfire Kick

Ki Repeater

A long-ranged but weak attack.
Tapping punch will launch up to 3 blasts in succession.

Ground Pounder

A powerful eruption of ki bursts from the ground around Garlic Jr. Hold kick to charge this attack and increase the distance of the energy bursts.

Power Kick

A fierce kick that is all about timing. Hold kick to charge for more damage, a fully-charged kick will allow you to perform Instantaneous Chain Combo.

Eternal Rage

Garlic Jr's little remaining sanity snaps, sending him into a violent rage doing massive damage. Grants one stack of super armor.

Supers:

Bash and Blast

Performs a more powerful version of Shoulder Bash and a follows it up with an intense energy blast.

Belly Beam

A strong, growing, long-ranged beam is fired from Garlic Jr's belly.

Rush and Crush

Garlic Jr goes nuts and performs a crazed, crushing combo.

Dead Zone

Garlic Jr opens a rift to the Dead Zone. Long charge time, unblockable. Grants 7 stacks of super armor. Garlic Jr himself will be consumed if he fails!


Chains and Fighting Tips:

1. When using the Ki Repeater special attack, continue tapping any 'P' button to chain up to 3 energy blasts!
2. Instantaneous Chain Combo (Teleport Attacks) can be used:
--after a successful Throw.
--after a successful Launcher
--after a successful, fully-charged Power Kick.
3. Avoid using power/energy consuming attacks when your power is low to avoid tiring out.
4. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
5. Attacks do less damage after each successful hit in a combo. After 10 hits you can only do 20% damage per attack. Supers are not affected by this.
6. Use air dash wisely, excessive use causes energy loss. When out of power you may only use a single air dash before landing.
7. Use Advance Guard if you are caught in a corner and only able to block.
8. Follow Hellfire Kick with Makyo Meteor for even heavier damage.
9. Follow Shoulder Bash with Garlic Dash and Smash for more damage.
10. Recover from certain falling states by pressing PP or KK.
