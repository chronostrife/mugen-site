Character Information:

Android 16 is an incomplete android that appeared in the main DBZ timeline. Unlike Android 17 and 18, 16 is not interested in fighting or destruction. He loves nature and only fights to protect these things, with the exception of Goku, of whom he is programmatically tasked with killing. Because the threat of Cell destroying the world is so great, he overrides his orders temporarily to assist Goku and company to fight the greater evil(see almost every other villain in DB/Z).

In the end 16 proves to be much stronger than 17 and 18. Capable of defeating Cell prior to his fusions. Perfect Cell eventually destroys 16 which consequently seals his own fate, cutting the last thread of Gohan's resistance to ascending to Super Saiyan 2. After Cell is defeated, nearly everyone who he had killed was brought back to life using the Dragon Balls . Unlike 17 and 18 however, 16 is never seen again, it is not known if 16 being fully machine was actually revived by the Dragon or not.



This Android 16 comes with a Mugen 1.0 version and a Mugen 1.1 version, while he was primarily designed for Mugen 1.1 compatability. I included a special Mugen 1.0 version which uses the original sprite set for 16 by Benhazard.

To activate the Mugen 1.0 version, add the following line to your 'data/select.def': "16_CStrife/16_CStrife1_0.def". The key difference between the two versions aside from sprites is the Mugen 1.0 version always fights at the highest difficult setting of 8(it is not affected by AILevel). The 1.0 version may not be affected by some of the Interpolate effects from the .AIR file, this includes rotations and fade effects on things like the bull casings from his machine gun arms.
The Mugen 1.0 version is Mugen 1.1 compatible as well, in case you want to include both versions of 16 in your Mugen 1.1 roster.

Chains and Fighting Tips:

1. Use the Rocket Punches and Ki Cannon in sequence for improved effect(faster Ki Cannon)!
2. The Rocket Punches use no power, but have a brief cooldown! Try and get a feel for them.
3. This Android 16 can power up like any other DBZ character!
4. Use Self-Destruct when the opponent is under 500(1/2) health.
5. During the scan sequence of Self Destruct, the player controls the reticle!
6. If Self Destruct fails, 16's head survives for a short time. You can only recover in training mode!
7. Teleport attack commands:
--Hold Forward+ Tap A+X buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Back+ Tap A+X buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Up + Tap A+X buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Down+ Tap A+X buttons simultaneously, This one cannot be chain with by other Teleport Attacks.
8. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
